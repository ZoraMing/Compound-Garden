## ğŸ“– Introduction

**Compound Garden** is an interactive exploration of compound interest inspired by the aesthetics of *Florence* and the interactivity of *The Evolution of Trust*. It visualizes the journey from saving pennies to achieving Financial Independence (FIRE).

### âœ¨ Features
- **Interactive Storytelling**: 5 chapters guiding you through linear growth, compound interest, and passive income.
- **Real-time Visualization**: Powered by Phaser, watch your wealth curve grow instantly as you tweak the sliders.
- **Bilingual**: Fully localized for both English and Chinese.
- **Sandbox Mode**: Unlocked after the story, allowing you to plan your own path to financial freedom.
